About the Project

GreenTex was developed for Infosys Hackathon 2025 to support sustainable textile supply chains. Our goal is to help SMEs adopt eco-conscious practices and connect with global markets.

Key Features

  • Real-time sustainability dashboards (IoT-based)
  • Automated fair trade compliance reports
  • Blockchain for transparency
  • Global marketplace access

Technology Stack

  • IoT
  • Blockchain
  • Cloud (AWS)
  • HTML
  • CSS

How It Helps SMEs

  • Affordable and easy to adopt
  • Helps meet international regulations
  • Opens export opportunities
  • Enables small manufacturers to participate in sustainable trade with ease

UN SDG Alignment

GreenTex aligns with SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure) by promoting sustainable practices and supporting innovation in textile manufacturing.
